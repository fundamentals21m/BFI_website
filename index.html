About the Author

Brian Hirschfield

CFA, ASA, FRM, CQF, Bitcoiner

With over two decades in financial risk management, including roles at some of the most storied institutions in finance, Brian brings a unique perspective to Bitcoin's institutional adoption.

His career spans actuarial science, quantitative finance, and enterprise risk management. After discovering Bitcoin, he dedicated two years to intensive study, ultimately building innovative strategies that form the basis of this book.

Brian hosts several podcasts and is committed to helping institutions understand and adopt Bitcoin effectively.
